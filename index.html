FAQ

How can I book a test drive on your website?

Booking a test drive is easy! Simply visit our homepage and enter the car model you are interested in. Click on the "Book Test Drive" button, and you'll see a list of available times. Choose the one that suits you best and follow the simple steps to complete your booking.

What should I do if I need to make changes to my car reservation?

Making changes to your car reservation is hassle-free. Log in to your account, go to the "My Reservations" section, and select the reservation you want to modify. From there, you can usually find options to edit dates, car preferences, or even cancel the reservation if needed.

Are there any specific documents required for financing options?

Stay informed about required documents for financing by checking our dedicated financing page. We provide up-to-date information on the necessary paperwork, credit requirements, and available finance plans for various models. It's essential to review this information before planning your purchase.

What payment methods are accepted for car purchases on your site?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit cards, debit cards, and popular digital wallets. During the purchase process, you can choose your preferred payment option. Rest assured, our payment gateway is secure and ensures the safety of your financial information.

How can I contact customer support for assistance during my car purchase?

We're here to help! You can reach our customer support team 24/7 through our helpline or via email. We also offer a live chat option on our website for quick assistance. If you encounter any issues during your purchase or have questions, don't hesitate to get in touch, and we'll do our best to assist you promptly.
